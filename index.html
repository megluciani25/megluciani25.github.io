Mistaken Feelings

poetry

This poem is written about a male counterpart that deserts the narrator, leaving her desolate. His relationship with her is never specified, leaving room for interpretation. However, the male figure in my mind is a father figure, rather than a romantic partner. It speaks of the narrator's heartbreak when he leaves her after having a positive impact on her life--specifically, helping her rebuild her fractured life. This piece describes her loss of him and how it has affected her, as well as her contemplation of what he thought of her. It concludes with her detailing the lasting impact he has on her life and how she hopes she will find friendship with him again.
